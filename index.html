Game Instructions

  • The game has 2 players, playing in rounds.
  • In each turn, a player rolls 2 dice as many times as he wishes.
  • Each result will get added to current player round’s score.
  • if the player rolls a double six all his round’s score gets lost. After that, it's the next player’s turn.
  • A player can choose to ‘Hold’, which means that his round’s score gets added to his global score. After that, it's the next player's turn.
  • The first player to reach target score points wins.
